# Tata iQ – AI Transformation Consulting Internship (Forage)

## Overview

This internship was a **job simulation with Tata iQ on the Forage platform**, focused on **AI transformation and data analytics in financial services**. The main goal was to help a collections team reduce **customer credit delinquency** by using **AI, data analysis, and responsible automation**.

I acted as an **AI Transformation Consultant**, learning to turn data insights into business-ready recommendations while ensuring **ethical, explainable, and regulator-friendly AI solutions**.

---

## Problem Statement

The Collections team faced these challenges:

* Difficulty identifying customers most at risk of default
* Reactive collections approach rather than **proactive, data-driven outreach**
* Ensuring AI decisions were **fair, explainable, and compliant**

The project’s goal was to design an **AI-powered strategy** that predicts delinquency and improves collection efficiency while maintaining fairness and compliance.

---

## Tasks and Work Done

### Task 1 – Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

**Goal:** Understand delinquency patterns and risk factors.

* Analyzed customer demographics, financial health, and payment behavior
* Identified key risk indicators like **credit utilization, missed payments, debt-to-income ratio**
* Summarized insights using GenAI tools

**Outcome:** Clear understanding of which customers are at risk and why.

---

### Task 2 – Predictive Model Planning

**Goal:** Design a transparent, reliable delinquency prediction framework.

* Conceptualized a **logistic regression-based risk model** (no-code)
* Selected high-impact features from EDA findings
* Planned evaluation metrics (Precision, Recall, F1-score, AUC) and fairness checks

**Outcome:** An **explainable, regulator-friendly predictive model** aligned with industry standards.

---

### Task 3 – Business Insights and Recommendations

**Goal:** Turn analytics into actionable insights for the Collections team.

* Segmented customers based on risk
* Created **SMART recommendations** (e.g., targeted outreach for high-risk customers)
* Assessed feasibility, ROI, and ethical impact

**Outcome:** A **stakeholder-ready report** linking data insights to measurable business actions.

---

### Task 4 – AI-Powered Autonomous Collections Strategy

**Goal:** Scale insights through responsible automation.

* Designed a **high-level AI-powered collections system**
* Defined what actions can be automated vs. need human oversight
* Built guardrails for **fairness, transparency, compliance, and explainability**
* Suggested KPIs to measure success

**Outcome:** A **scalable and ethical AI collections framework** ready for executive review.
